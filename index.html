How to Play

Select Level: Choose between Beginner, Intermediate, Expert

Select Time: Choose a Time to add Difficulty to Level

Rules

    Fill in the Boxes with alternative Number & Letters

    Every time you choose an incorrect number or letter you loose a life

    When all lives are used up GAME OVER!

    Depending on the time you set yourself, if the time runs outs before you complete the puzzle GAME OVER!
